ECOLOGICAL INSIGHTS
-------------------
- Species prefers cold, high-altitude environments (optimal ~0°C)
- Strongly associated with mountain ranges (Himalayas, Karakoram, Pamir, Tian Shan)
- Temperature is the most important predictor (46% contribution)
- Habitat forms corridors along major mountain systems
- Potentially vulnerable to climate change due to temperature dependency
